### Week 5 Objectives:

* Master authorization mechanisms, Identity and Access Management (IAM), and data security (KMS) on AWS.
* Practice resource automation (using Lambda to manage EC2, integrating with Slack) and system monitoring (CloudTrail, Athena).

* Proficiently operated authorization and account management via IAM (Policies, Roles, Switch Roles).
